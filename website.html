Website

A place to send customers

From design and content to ongoing maintenance, Enabled helps your business look right and give customers what they need to choose you. Here’s how we can help.

What we offer

Design

We create the look, structure and user experience of your site so it reflects your brand and makes it easy for visitors to find what they need. This includes layout, navigation, visual style and responsive behaviour across devices.

Dr Katelyn Tadd

Dr Katelyn Tadd needed a basic website that would act as a place for referring doctors and patients to get in touch. With Dr Tadd's input, Enabled designed and rolled out the content you see on the website.

Content creation

We write and organise the words, images and other assets that go on your site. That might mean new copy for key pages, blog posts, FAQs, event listings or product descriptions — all tuned to your audience and goals.

On Sound

On Sound asked Enabled to create website copy that would explain exactly what they do whilst leveraging some key phrases to improve SEO.

Maintenance

We keep your site running smoothly and up to date. That includes technical updates, security and performance checks, plus content updates such as news, events, team changes or seasonal offers so your site stays current.

Work Experience Kid Podcast

The Work Experience Kid is a podcast that talks to guests across a number of industries. Enabled was engaged to update the content each week when a new episode dropped. The "Latest Episode" would be updated along with links to hear the episode along with a synopsis.
